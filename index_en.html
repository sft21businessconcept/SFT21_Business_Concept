Explanation of the Bonus Program for License Owners

PHASE 1 BONUS and PHASE 2 BONUS

By purchasing one or more selected License Packages, you gain the right to participate in the Bonus Program, which opens up the possibility of earning bonuses in two phases, depending on the company's business success.

Bonus Distribution Model

In order to commence with the distribution of bonuses within the projected period of 3 years from the date of signing the License Purchase Agreement (or sooner, if possible), the Company will, upon the completion of software development and the launch of the SFT21 Business Concept, set aside funds for the Bonus Distribution Fund in each accounting period (28 days).

  • a minimum of 50% of the monthly Net Profit
  • 100% of the revenue generated by the Ambassador positions (32 accounts on the 5th level of the SFalgorithm)
  • 100% of the revenue generated by vacant State positions (approx. 170 accounts on the 6th and 7th levels of the SFalgorithm)

Additionally, by purchasing a License, you get an account that takes a position in the SFalgorithm, with which you participate in the distribution of funds. Funds received from the Friend Bonus go into your Wallet with the aim of expediting the payment of potential bonuses.

After the payment of the Phase 1 Bonus, the Company will continue to allocate a minimum of 21% of Net Profit to the Fund each accounting period (28 days) until the Phase 2 Bonus is also paid out.

If business development, the sale of ownership shares, or other circumstances lead to the possibility of faster payment of total bonuses, the Company will allocate more funds or will pay out the entire amount even before the projected deadline.

Each License Owner receives a potential bonus according to the terms of the License Purchase Agreement.

IT IS IMPORTANT TO NOTE AND CLEARLY EMPHASIZE THAT THE POTENTIAL BONUS IS AN AMOUNT THAT, ACCORDING TO ESTIMATES AND REALISTIC PROJECTIONS, YOU COULD RECEIVE WITHIN A PERIOD OF THREE TO FIVE YEARS FROM THE DATE OF SIGNING THE LICENSE PURCHASE AGREEMENT, BUT NO ONE CAN GUARANTEE THIS TIMEFRAME WITH CERTAINTY.

REFERRAL BONUS (REFERRAL PROGRAM)

To make information about the SFT21 Business Concept and the opportunity to purchase a License available to as many potential associates as possible, a Referral Bonus has been introduced as an additional incentive.

Every existing registered user and License Owner who refers a new Buyer is entitled to the Referral Bonus.

Based on your successful referral, i.e., the purchase of a License Package by your referred person, you are entitled to a referral bonus of 10% (ten percent) of the paid License price.

It is important to note that the total amount earned from the referral bonus cannot exceed ten (10) times the price of your initially purchased License.

For example, if you as a License Owner purchase LP3 for a price of €500, the maximum referral bonus amount you can earn is €5,000 (10 x €500).

By the same logic, if you bring in a new Buyer who purchases a license for, say, €100,000, out of a possible 10% referral bonus (€10,000), you would only receive €5,000 due to your existing limit.

However, to avoid losing part of the potential bonus, you have the option to increase your own limit by purchasing additional Licenses before your referred buyer makes a larger payment.

The amount earned through the referral bonus will be visible in your user account in the SFoffice and available for use after the upgrade and launch of the core SFT21 software.

The earned referral bonus amount is distributed in a 50% ratio to your “Wallet” and 50% to your “Webshop Wallet”.

Funds in the “Wallet” can be used at your discretion: withdraw them via available payment processors, use them to pay for products and services within the Company's system, or transfer them to another person.

Funds in the “Webshop Wallet” can be used exclusively for purchasing products and services from business partners in the SFshop or can be sent to another registered person.

What is a subscription period?

A subscription period (SP) is a time frame that lasts 28 days and always begins on the following Monday after a Starter Package has been purchased.

By selecting a Starter Package, you pay for a certain number of subscription periods, which determines your earning limits, the number of SFpoints, and the number of SFcodes.

To actively participate and earn bonus income (friendship, sponsor, and rebate bonuses), you need to have an active account, meaning a paid subscription period. The direct bonus can be received even with an inactive account, provided that you have completed your work obligations.

If, upon the expiration of the last paid SP, you do not pay for the next one and have no funds in your reserve wallet (RW) to pay for it, your account becomes inactive. After that, friendship, sponsor, and rebate bonuses go 100% to the RW, but you retain the right to the direct bonus if you earn it. If you have an inactive account, the reserve wallet collects the necessary funds to pay for the next SP, which will be automatically activated when the required amount is collected (on the following Monday), provided that you have completed your work obligations. If you have an inactive account and have not completed your work obligations, the collected and blocked funds from the reserve wallet will be deducted by the system and forwarded to the company's automatic wallet (AW), which is tasked with returning the funds back into distribution to active users via system accounts (SA).

After you have used all the subscription periods included in your Starter Package, you can top up with new SPs in one of the following ways:

  • by transferring funds via payment processors and paying for new subscription periods
  • by transferring funds from another user's account and paying for new subscription periods
  • by collecting funds from bonus earnings in the Reserve Wallet (the system automatically renews the subscription)

Within one year, you can have a maximum of 13 active subscription periods, provided there are no inactive days between the subscription periods.

After the last 21st subscription period has passed, which means the SFT21 franchise is fully paid, the user no longer pays for a subscription, and the subscription periods become accounting periods (AP).

As a free registered user, can I earn a Starter Package?

Access to and use of our platform and its associated applications is FREE, provided that you verify your identity and create your own SFnet social network page.

Yes... as a free registered user, you have the option, at your own discretion, to participate in expanding our community by registering new free users, and in two ways, you can earn a Starter Package and the right to participate in the company's profit distribution.

FIRST WAY

After you register your 10th free user, provided that all of them have been verified and have opened their own page on the SFnet social network, the system will automatically activate a so-called "SPfree" Starter Package for you at the first available position in the SFalgorithm.

From that moment, you have seven days to accept or decline the account activation.

If you agree to the account activation, your earnings limit will be set to 50 SFC, you will receive 210 SFpoints (21 points for each member), you will get an additional SFcode (in addition to the first one you received with your free registration, you now have 2), and you will continue your business according to the rules of the franchise agreement.

If you decline the account activation, the system will remove your account from the SFalgorithm position, reset the algorithm, and you will continue as a free registered user with no obligations.

If you do not decide within seven days, the system will remove your account from the SFalgorithm position, reset the algorithm, and you will continue as a free registered user with no obligations.

SECOND WAY

As a free registered user, you can register one or more free users.

The moment the first, or any of your free registered users, decides to activate their account by purchasing a Starter Package, as a final step, the system will offer them the choice to select a new sponsor or to keep you, the referrer, as their sponsor.

If they choose a new sponsor, you remain their referrer, but they continue their business with another member, who, as their sponsor, is entitled to direct and sponsor bonuses.

If they choose you, the system will automatically occupy the first two available positions in the SFalgorithm. Your user account will be placed in the first position, and theirs in the second.

As in the first case, from that moment, you have seven days to accept or decline the activation of your account in the SFalgorithm.

If you agree to the account activation, your earnings limit will be set to 50 SFC, you will receive a minimum of 21 SFC (or more, depending on the Starter Package your member chooses), 21 SFpoints, you will get an additional SFcode (you now have 2), and you will continue your business according to the rules of the franchise agreement.

If you decline the account activation, the system will remove your account from the SFalgorithm position, reset the algorithm, and you will continue as a free registered user with no obligations.

If you do not decide within seven days, the system will remove your account from the SFalgorithm position, reset the algorithm, and you will continue as a free registered user with no obligations.
